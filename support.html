Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use Surging?
No. Surging works entirely on your device with no account, no sign-in, and no cloud storage. Your goals and progress stay private on your phone.
How does the AI coaching work?
Surging uses Groq, a fast cloud AI service, to analyze your goal progress and offer coaching suggestions. AI insights work out of the box with no setup. You can optionally add your own free Groq API key from console.groq.com for unlimited requests.
What is the Ollama option in Settings?
Ollama is an advanced, optional feature for users who want to run AI fully locally on their own Mac with complete privacy. It requires a Mac running the Ollama software on the same network. Most users can ignore this and use the default Groq option.
How do I move my data to a new device?
Go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Export backup. Share the file to your new device (via AirDrop, Files, or email), then open Surging on the new device and choose Import backup.
How do reminders work?
In Settings you can enable a daily reminder at a time and on the days you choose. You can also turn on work session tracking, which sends hourly check-ins when you're falling behind your pace during a set work window.
How do I earn points and ranks?
You earn points for hitting milestones: +5 at 50% of a goal, +10 for completing it, bonus points for streaks, and +5 for a perfect day. Points move you up an 8-level rank ladder from Rookie to Legend.
Is my data backed up automatically?
Data is stored locally on your device and persists across app updates and restarts. It is not backed up to the cloud automatically. Use the Export backup feature to save a copy you control.
